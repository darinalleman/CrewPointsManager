About Us

The Shippensburg University Cycling Club is a club sport whose members compete in road, cyclocross and mountain bike events in the Eastern Collegiate Cycling Conference.

While school is in session, we have a number of rides throughout the week aimed at any and all skill levels. During the summer we host the South Mountain Summer Smackdown race series which is a low key, unofficial, but fun set of races throughout the summer all over the South Mountain.
