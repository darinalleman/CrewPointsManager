IRON MASTERS #3

The Ironmaster's Time Trial begins and ends at the historic Ironmaster's Mansion in Pine Grove Furnace State Park. An out-and-back course (five miles out, five miles back) though towering pines and curvy roads, with two gut-wrenching kickers to start and end the ride. Start time is nearly always 6:00. The cost is always exactly $0. Ample parking at the mansion or the Appalachian Trail store area next door which, incidentally, will be all too happy to sell you a hamburger, ice cream cone, or cold Coca-Cola after your effort.
