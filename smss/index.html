THE SOUTH MOUNTAIN SUMMER SMACKDOWN

The South Mountain Summer Smackdown (SMSS) was conceived (long before my time in south-central) as a friendly summer club challenge between South Mountain area bicycle clubs.

Today, the SMSS is both an individual and club competition--you show up, your results are recorded, points tabulated, and at the end of the season there are some winners. This is a totally low-key, informal competition. We are not racing, we are "racing." These are not events, they are "events." No entry fees. No waivers. Just show up and ride your bike fast.

NEW IN 2018: For all mass-start "events," ad hoc teams (of 3-4) will be formed, handicapped to keep things interesting. Riders will score points for the series overall based on the finishing position of the top rider on their ad hoc team.

Start time is 6:30 pm unless otherwise announced. (If you're riding to and from, that means you may want to bring lights.) Parking is noted in the course description for each "event."

Rally your teammates, riding buddies, frienemies, whomever... And spread the word! If you know someone who should be on my email list, please pass on their contact info.
